At HICO Helium & Balloons of Dallas we service the greater Dallas / Fort Worth Metroplex area with rental of compressed helium gas cylinders for both business and individual customers.  Counter service or same day1 delivery is available for all rental customers.  We have 3 tank sizes to choose from to meet your helium needs.

Permanent Accounts

Permanent account customers keep 1 or more helium cylinders on their premises for 6 months or more.  Tank refills are provided for the standard fill charge through either same day delivery or counter service.  Permanent account customers are provided free delivery services with a minimum order of $50.00 product only.  A $10.00 haz mat fee for each delivery or pick up will be charged.

Temporary Accounts

Temporary account customers are provided short term filled helium gas cylinder rentals for periods of less than 2 weeks.  Monthly tank rental policies apply to any tanks held over 2 weeks.  Delivery service3 is available for a $25 delivery fee and $25 pickup fee. $10 is added to all pickups and deliveries.
